# Cybersecurity Governance & Risk Analysis — SpaceTech Corp

## Overview
A governance and risk assessment of a fictional satellite operator (SpaceTech Corp). Using dashboard evidence, this analysis identifies governance and strategic gaps, evaluates their risk impact, and delivers prioritized recommendations across four domains.

## Domains Assessed
- Governance & Leadership
- Risk Management
- Policy & Compliance
- Technology Enablement

## Approach
Each observation is traced from technical evidence to its governance gap, organizational risk impact, and a strategic recommendation with assigned ownership and reporting cadence. A separate priority mapping ranks each signal as Critical or High.

## Skills Demonstrated
- Connecting technical findings to business and regulatory consequences
- Building governance mechanisms: KRIs/KPIs, escalation triggers, risk tolerance, board reporting
- Risk triage and prioritization
- Writing actionable recommendations with clear accountability
